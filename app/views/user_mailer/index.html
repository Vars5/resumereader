Be a Peer Mentor leader for 2011!



Applications are open for UNSW Business Society Peer Mentors in 2011!

 We're looking for keen Business students who are in 2nd year or above in 2011 to be Mentors to first year Business Students next year in Semester 1.

You will work with a partner Mentor to guide a group of 8-10 students through their first few weeks of being at UNSW by giving them helpful tips about things you wish you had known when you were a first year and bond with them through group activities.

Being a BSOC Peer Mentor looks fantastic on your Resume and Secondary Transcript and gives you the opportunity to help out first year Business students as they make the transition from high school to university!

Apply here now!

Short interviews with the Education Directors will be conducted after Mid-Semester Break in Semester 2, 2010.
